What happened: I dropped my 2026 Tesla Model X with the valet at VanCity Parking (359 9th Ave, New York, NY) earlier that day, at approximately 10:30 AM. When I returned for the car later that evening, the valet brought it up for pickup and attempted to move it from its position behind another customer's vehicle (a black Mercedes-Benz SUV). Instead of putting the car in Reverse to back out, the valet placed it in Drive and slammed on the accelerator, launching the Tesla forward and rear-ending the Mercedes directly in front of it. The collision is captured on the Tesla's built-in front dashcam, which was actively recording at the time. The impact caused damage to the hood, scratches to the front nose, and broke the front Tesla emblem free of its mounting clip.
